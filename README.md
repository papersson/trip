## Day Trips & Highlights

### From Santiago (Feb 8)
- **Cajon del Maipo**: Embalse El Yeso, Banos Colina hot springs

### From San Pedro (Feb 11–12)
- **El Tatio Geysers**: 4am departure, 1.5h drive, 4,300m altitude
- **Valle de la Luna**: Sunset visit, 20min from San Pedro

### En Route (Feb 13–14)
- **Mano del Desierto**: Giant hand sculpture, stop between San Pedro and Antofagasta
- **Pan de Azucar NP**: Coastal desert, cacti, guanacos - morning stop before Bahia Inglesa

### From La Serena (Feb 16–17)
- **Isla Damas**: Boat trip - penguins, dolphins, sea lions (1.5h from La Serena)
- **Valle del Elqui**: Pisco distilleries, Vicuna (1h from La Serena)

### From Cajon del Maipo (Feb 18–19)
- **Feb 18**: Drive from La Serena (5h), day in Santiago, then to San Alfonso
- **Feb 19**: Full day in mountains
  - **Banos Morales**: Natural thermal baths at 1,850m altitude, open 24h (30min from San Alfonso)
  - Afternoon: thermal baths
- **Feb 20**: Return early to Santiago, fly home
